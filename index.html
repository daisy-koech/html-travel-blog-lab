My Adventures in Fire Island

The Journey

My journey to Fire Island started with a two-hour drive to the harbour port, followed by another two hours via a ferry to the Fire Island.

Something about travelling by water changes the pace of everything. Time seems to slow down, wind brushing my face and the rhythmical sound of waves.

After two hours, the island finally came into a clearer view. I was in awe. From a distance it had looked beautiful, but seeing it up close, it felt surreal.

Highlights of the Trip

One of the most memorable parts of the trip was simply walking barefooted along the shoreline. The sound of waves made everything feel slower and more peaceful.

Another highlight was exploring the island itself.Aside from the chirping of birds, numerous scents of flowers and well-built walking trails, there was the landscape. It was breathtaking.

The sunsets of course were unforgettable. Watching the sun disappear slowly into the horizon while the ocean reflected the colors of the sky was a priceless moment.
